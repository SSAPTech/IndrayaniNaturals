Garam Masala - Detailed Information

About This Blend

Our Garam Masala is a traditional blend of warming spices that adds depth and complexity to your dishes. Each spice is carefully selected and roasted to perfection before being ground together.

Complete Ingredients

  • Black Peppercorns
  • Green Cardamom Pods
  • Cinnamon Sticks
  • Cloves
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Mace
  • Bay Leaves
  • Nutmeg

Fun Facts

  • The name "Garam Masala" means "hot spice mix" in Hindi
  • Each family in India has their own unique recipe
  • Traditionally made fresh for each use
  • Contains warming spices that help digestion

Best Used In

  • Curries and gravies
  • Rice dishes
  • Marinades
  • Soups and stews
Biryani Masala - Detailed Information

About This Blend

Our Biryani Masala is specially crafted to create the perfect aromatic biryani. The blend of spices creates layers of flavor that make your biryani truly special.

Complete Ingredients

  • Cumin Seeds
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Star Anise
  • Black Cardamom
  • Green Cardamom
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon
  • Bay Leaves
  • Mace

Fun Facts

  • Biryani originated in the royal kitchens of the Mughal Empire
  • Each region in India has its own unique biryani style
  • The word "biryani" comes from the Persian word "birian"
  • Traditionally cooked in sealed pots to trap the aromas

Best Used In

  • Chicken Biryani
  • Vegetable Biryani
  • Pulao
  • Rice-based dishes
