Garam Masala - Detailed Information

About This Blend

Our Garam Masala is a traditional blend of warming spices that adds depth and complexity to your dishes. Each spice is carefully selected and roasted to perfection before being ground together.

Complete Ingredients

  • Chilli
  • Coconut
  • Black pepper
  • Cumin seeds
  • Cloves
  • Mace
  • Black stone flower
  • Star anise
  • Cinnamon
  • Black cardamom
  • Green cardamom
  • Bay leaf
  • Indian rose chestnut
  • Tailed pepper
  • Asafoetida
  • Coriander
  • Turmeric
  • Dry ginger
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Oil

Fun Facts

  • The name "Garam Masala" means "hot spice mix" in Hindi
  • Each family in India has their own unique recipe
  • Traditionally made fresh for each use
  • Contains warming spices that help digestion

Best Used In

  • Curries and gravies
  • Rice dishes
  • Marinades
  • Soups and stews

Storage Instructions

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep in an airtight container for maximum freshness.

Mungvadi - Detailed Information

About This Blend

Unleash the authentic flavors of India with Mungvadi—a culinary gem crafted from sun-kissed mung dal and an aromatic blend of spices. Each bite of these protein-rich mungvadis delivers a symphony of taste, featuring the gentle warmth of chili powder, the earthy notes of coriander and cumin, the pungency of garlic, and a hint of turmeric and asafoetida, all harmonized with a touch of salt.

Complete Ingredients

  • Mungdal
  • Chilli powder
  • Coriander
  • Garlic
  • Cumin seeds
  • Turmeric
  • Asafoetida
  • Salt

Fun Facts

  • Mungvadi is a traditional Maharashtrian delicacy
  • Rich in protein and fiber
  • Often used as a meat substitute in vegetarian dishes

Best Used In

  • Traditional Indian Curries
  • Vegetable Dishes
  • Gravy Preparations
  • Rice Dishes

Storage Instructions

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep in an airtight container for maximum freshness.

Fun Facts
