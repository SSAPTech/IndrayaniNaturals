Hyderabadi Biryani

Aromatic rice dish with tender meat and fragrant spices, perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g chicken/mutton
  • 2 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Fresh mint and coriander
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 4 green chilies
  • 1 cup ghee
Method:
  1. Marinate meat with Biryani Masala, yogurt, and spices for 2 hours
  2. Cook rice until 70% done with whole spices
  3. Layer rice and meat in a heavy pot with fried onions and mint
  4. Seal the pot with dough and cook on low heat for 30 minutes
  5. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving
Tips:
  • Use aged Basmati rice for better aroma
  • Don't skip the resting time
  • Serve with raita and salad

Spicy Chicken Curry

Rich and flavorful curry with our special Garam Masala, perfect with rice or roti.

Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken
  • 2 tbsp Garam Masala
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • Fresh ginger and garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
Method:
  1. Heat oil and saut√© onions until golden brown
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes
  3. Add Garam Masala and cook briefly to release flavors
  4. Add chicken and cook until tender
  5. Finish with tomato puree and simmer for 10 minutes
Tips:
  • Use bone-in chicken for better flavor
  • Cook on low heat for tender meat
  • Garnish with fresh coriander

Spiced Yellow Dal

Comforting lentil dish with aromatic spices, a perfect side dish for any meal.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup yellow lentils
  • 1 tbsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tbsp Cumin Powder
  • 2 green chilies
  • Fresh coriander
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
Method:
  1. Wash and cook lentils with turmeric until soft
  2. Heat ghee and add mustard seeds
  3. Add cumin and chilies for tempering
  4. Pour tempering over cooked dal
  5. Simmer for 5 minutes
Tips:
  • Don't overcook the lentils
  • Use fresh ghee for better flavor
  • Serve hot with rice or roti

Coriander Chutney

Fresh and zesty chutney with our Coriander Powder, perfect as a dip or spread.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups fresh coriander
  • 1 tbsp Coriander Powder
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
Method:
  1. Wash and roughly chop coriander
  2. Blend all ingredients until smooth
  3. Add water for desired consistency
  4. Season with salt and lemon juice
  5. Refrigerate for 30 minutes
Tips:
  • Use fresh coriander for best flavor
  • Adjust spice level to taste
  • Store in airtight container
